The solution contours illustration foL1 (Lasso) and L2 (rigid) regularizations constrained in two dimensions, where Lasso contour has a diagonal distance equal to t and rigid optimum has a circular contour with radius is t.  The square error function Obj (which is perpendicular to the contour) obtains optimum solution when touching the contour. Compare to rigid regularization, Lasso has a sharp edge, which results in the Obj function more likely to contact Lasso contour at axes, this in turn the elementmatrix w more easily to fall onto axes hence produce more sparse network.
